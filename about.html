Hello!

Welcome to Vega’s World! Vega is a miniature pinscher, and is currently 5 years old. Her favorite activities to pass the time are taking a walk, chewing her bones, and sleeping in on the weekends. Her favorite food is beef, but she will probably eat anything if she is hungry enough. Don’t ever let her know when you are going out without taking her -- she will throw a fit when you come back. Most important of all, make sure you give her lots of attention (or, “Vega lovin”). She loves to be petted on her stomach and behind the ears.

Using HTML and CSS, this website was created so that everyone can enjoy the presence of Vega! Click around our website just to learn more about this awesome dog. Make sure to click the subscribe button on our front page for free photos of Vega. Also, drop by our store for some premium content. You can purchase the best snapshots taken of Vega, and you can also buy yourself some petting sessions with her. For just $40, you can have a whole 90 minutes with her! Now that is a steal. Lastly, visit our contact section if you would like to reach out to the owner regarding anything, whether it be about our website or just questions about Vega in general.

Thank you for visiting Vega’s World. We hope you enjoy your stay!
